SON CHAEYOUNG

Son Chae-young (Hangul: 손채영), better known by her first name Chaeyoung (Hangul: 채영), is a South Korean singer, rapper, songwriter and dancer. A member of the girl group Twice, she was assigned the role of the group's main rapper. Since Twice’s debut, Chaeyoung adds a fierce and playful energy to her verses. She is also an avid artist who has drawn the cover art for one of Twice’s limited edition albums, as well as sketches of her fellow members.
