CHOU TZUYU

Chou Tzuyu (Hangul: 주쯔위), best known by her stage name, Tzuyu (Hangul: 쯔위), is a Taiwanese singer and dancer, currently based in South Korea. She is a member of Twice, as a lead dancer, sub-vocalist, visual, and the maknae. Tzuyu is Twice’s “maknae” and was only 16 at the time of the group’s debut. While she first stood out for her striking appearance, Tzuyu has grown into a poised performer.
