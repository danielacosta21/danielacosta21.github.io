ICSM CO.

ICSM CO. is an online website containing an information guide on the K-Pop girl group TWICE. It is managed by a team of TWICE fans (or ONCEs) who are still currently in high school and just aim to provide an accessible starter guide for everyone who wants to get to know them. ICSM CO. and the handlers of the page are not in any way officially recognized by nor affiliated with TWICE and JYP Entertainment.

FUN FACT! ICSM also stands for "I Can't Stop Me," which is the title track of TWICE's album "Eyes Wide Open."
