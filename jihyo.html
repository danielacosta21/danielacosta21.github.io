Park Jihyo

Park Ji-hyo (Hangul: 박지효, born as Park Ji-soo), better known by her first name, Jihyo (Hangul: 지효), is a South Korean singer and dancer, and is the leader and main vocalist of Twice. Jihyo became a K-pop idol trainee in elementary school and trained for 10 years before debuting in Twice. She mostly has the key lines in the choruses of Twice’s tracks. In addition to a strong voice that rarely wavers in live performances, Jihyo is known for her versatile dance skills and overall charisma.
