IM NAYEON

Im Na-yeon (Hangul: 임나연) , better known by her stage name Nayeon (Hangul: 나연), is a South Korean singer. She is the oldest member, lead vocalist and face of the girl group Twice. One of the strongest vocalists in the group, Nayeon frequently starts Twice’s songs and delivers the chorus measures. Nayeon is the first member of TWICE debuted as a solo artist , with her album IM NAYEON on June 24, 2022.
