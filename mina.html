MYOUI MINA

Mina Myoui (Hangul: 묘이 미나, commonly known as Mina (Hangul: 미나), is a Japanese singer. She is a member of Twice as a sub-vocalist and the main dancer. The only member of Twice to be born in the U.S. She trained in ballet for more than a decade, which may explain her elegant and serene poise, evident whether she is performing in a music video or speaking at an interview.
