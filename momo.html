HIRAI MOMO

Momo Hirai (Hangul: 히라이), better known as Momo (Hangul: 모모), is a Japanese singer and dancer, active in South Korea. She is the third oldest member of the K-pop girl group, Twice, as the main dancer, vocalist, and sub-rapper. Momo is not only viewed as the best dancer in Twice but one of the best dancers across all female K-pop acts.
